Discover the Breathtaking Hulunbuir

Escape the concrete jungle and encounter China's purest paradise.

How long has it been since you saw the true horizon?
In Hulunbuir, time seems to slow down. This is not just a trip, but a homecoming that lets your soul take a deep breath.

Unmissable Highlights

Gallop Freely · World-Class Pristine Prairie

As far as the eye can see, it is an ocean of green. Under the eternal blue sky, watch cattle and sheep scattered like pearls, and listen to the melodious sound of the horsehead fiddle. Here, you can ride a horse across the plains and be a free nomad for a day.

Emerald Wonderland · Asia's No.1 Wetland

The winding Genhe River nurtures the vibrant Ergun Wetland. Stroll through the romantic birch forests, breathe the freshest air of the Greater Khingan Mountains, and marvel at a landscape as beautiful as an oil painting.

Vast Waters · The 'Pearl' of the Prairie

The boundless Hulun Lake boasts a magnificence reminiscent of the ocean. Sit by the lake, feel the gentle breeze, watch waterbirds skim the surface, and savor the ultimate delicacy of the renowned 'Whole Fish Feast'.

Exotic Symphony · Fairytale Border City

Strolling through the streets of Manzhouli, the Russian faces and Byzantine architecture weave a rich exotic atmosphere. Find your inner child at the giant Matryoshka Square and experience the romantic fusion of China, Russia, and Mongolia.




5 Unmissable Experiences

  • 🌟 Sleep in a Starry Yurt: Fall asleep to the Milky Way in the heart of the prairie, and wake up to the first rays of sunlight and the sounds of sheep.
  • 🔥 Join a Bonfire Party: Dance the traditional Andai dance around a blazing bonfire with hospitable Mongolian locals.
  • 🍖 Feast on Authentic Meat: Tear into sizzling roast whole lamb, taste mellow hand-held meat broth, and savor authentic prairie flavors.
  • 🦌 Meet the Reindeer: Walk into the Aoluguya Evenki reindeer village and interact closely with the gentle elves of the forest.
  • 🚙 The Ultimate Border Drive: Drive along the Sino-Russian border highway, with Russian exotic scenery on your left and China's magnificent landscapes on your right.

The world is vast, but Hulunbuir is a must-see.

Whether you want to wander alone or share the joy with loved ones, the best scenery is already waiting for you here.
